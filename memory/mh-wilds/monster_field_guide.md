# Monster Hunter Wilds Monster Field Guide

Last refreshed: 2026-05-09

This is a compact field-guide memory for build prep and hunt routing. It lists the 34 large monsters represented through the TU4 / Ver. 1.041 data window, excluding Arch-Tempered, Tempered, and Frenzied state variants as separate monsters. For exact hitzone stars, break rewards, carve percentages, or post-patch additions, verify in-game or with current sources before making a final claim.

## General Field-Guide Use

- Build prep starts with element and key break targets, then matchup survival: resists, Blight Resistance, Stun Resistance, Guard/Guard Up, Evade comfort, or Guts/Tenacity.
- Guardian variants mimic their base monster but add Guardian crystal behavior and are immune to exhaust in current guide data.
- Title update monsters in this guide: Mizutsune (TU1), Lagiacrus and Seregios (TU2), Omega Planetes (TU3), and Gogmazios (TU4).
- Tempered monsters are your Artian material lane. Treat them as high-damage versions first, farming targets second.
- For bowguns, cross-check the actual shot hitzones in-game; "recommended element" does not always mean elemental ammo beats a strong raw/Pierce plan.
