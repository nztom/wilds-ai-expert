# Monster Hunter Wilds Fishing Locations

Last refreshed: 2026-05-07

This note broadens the repo from build-only advice into general Monster Hunter Wilds field knowledge. Use it when the user asks where to catch fish, what bait to use, or how to complete Kanya's fishing quests.

## Bait Unlocks And Use

- Common Wood Minnow: starting fishing lure from Kanya.
- Golden Bughead: from Kanya's Goldenfish quest; use for gaudy/gold-value fish such as Goldenfish, Goldenfry, and Platinumfish.
- Emerald Jitterbait: from Kanya's Gravid Bowfin quest; use for useful fish such as Sushifish, Whetfish, Gravid Bowfin, Gunpowderfish, Bomb Arowana, Burst Arowana, and Blind Perch.
- Tuff Joint Bait: from Kanya's whopper quest; use for swift/large fish such as Speartuna, Great Trevally, and Gastronome Tuna.
- Duster Rig: from Kanya's whopper quest; use for dark/muddy or bottom-feeding fish such as Gajau and Petricanths.
- Tentacle Jig: use for feeler/tentacle fish such as Escunite, Grand Escunite, and Goliath Squid.

## Practical Routes

- Goldenfish / Platinumfish farming: Scarlet Forest Area 16 Ruin Interior or Area 12 waterfall with Golden Bughead.
- Whopper farming: Scarlet Forest Area 17 Great Lake Shore. Use Tuff Joint Bait for Tuna / Trevally / Speartuna, Duster Rig for Gajau, Tentacle Jig at night for squid/escunites.
- Gravid Bowfin quest: try Iceshard Cliffs Area 20 Ancient Room pop-up camp or Ruins of Wyveria Area 13 Hidden Cave, then use Emerald Jitterbait.
- Nightflower Pollen is not a fish, but it supports Gold Melding Ticket trades: gather from Ephemeral Blossom during full moon at Scarlet Forest Area 18 and the Area 17/14 honey-vine connection. See `material_locations.md` for the canonical decoration-farming note.
