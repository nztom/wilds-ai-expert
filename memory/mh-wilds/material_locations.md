## Decoration Farming

Refreshed: 2026-05-07

- Fastest targeted high-tier decoration loop: use Wyverian Melding at Suja for Ancient Orb - Sword / Ancient Orb - Armor. Game8 lists the costs as 120 melding points for Ancient Orb - Sword and 60 for Ancient Orb - Armor; Gold Melding Tickets from Nightflower Pollen / item trading are high-value fuel.
- Nightflower Pollen comes from Ephemeral Blossom during full moon conditions. Current location notes list Windward Plains Areas 3 and 10, and Scarlet Forest Area 18 plus the Area 17/14 honey-vine connection. Some guides shorthand that second Scarlet Forest spot as Area 14; in practice, use the detailed map filter for Ephemeral Blossom once conditions are active.
- Fastest hunt-based loop: run High Rank 7-8 star Field Surveys / Investigations that visibly list Ancient Orb - Sword or Ancient Orb - Armor as bonus rewards. Prefer short multi-monster or easy tempered investigations when the reward panel has multiple Ancient Orbs.
- Ancient Orb - Armor starts appearing from High Rank 6-star Field Surveys / Investigations and is specifically shown as a 7-8 star Field Survey / Investigation reward. Ancient Orb - Sword is also available from some quest rewards, including the event quest "That Won't Faze Me!" and optional quest "When the Dust Settles" in Game8's listing.
- Ver. 1.041 made many event quests permanent, but not every event is a high-tier deco farm. Check the reward preview first; guaranteed Ancient Orb rewards beat vague "decoration" wording when farming specific high-rarity jewels.

## Special Armor Unlocks

Refreshed: 2026-05-07

- Clerk α / Clerk Vambraces α: unlocked from Grand Hub Arena quests, specifically by obtaining a Rathian Coin from Arena: Rathian. Clerk Vambraces α crafting materials are Blangonga Pelt+ x2, Pinnacle Coin x1, Rathian Coin x2, Chata Coin x1, and 4000z. Grand Hub / Arena access requires High Rank progression; Shacknews notes HR16 and speaking with Tetsuzan at Suja for Grand Hub access. The "New Fashion, Old Feelings" Smithy side mission requires the Type 1 / male visual version of Clerk Vambraces α, shown by the green dot in the forge list, not the orange diamond Type 2 version. Both can appear under the same armor name, so verify the icon on the crafted/layered variant. User in-game check, 2026-05-07: Gemma's quest detection also required turning the undershirt on. If Gemma still does not recognize the outfit after equipping the correct pieces, enable undershirt visibility, then untrack/retrack the side mission or reload/change area to refresh quest detection.

## General Material Rules

- Monster materials: hunt/capture/carve the named monster; quest rewards and investigation rewards are usually the reliable route for rarer parts.
- Bones/ore/gathering materials: use the in-game map filters and set waypoints to gathering nodes.
- Vendor/trade materials: check Nata, village intermediaries, Wudwud trades, and settlement-specific facilities.
- Artian weapon parts: tempered monsters and Suja melding are the core sources.
- Artian reinforcement materials: Azuz / Smelting Foundry systems, Nata, dismantling Artian weapons, and high-end quest rewards.

## Artian Reinforcement Materials

Used for Artian weapon reinforcement:

- Drearisite: 20 reinforcement points.
- Specklite: 100 reinforcement points.
- Argecite: 200 reinforcement points.
- Oricalcite: 300 reinforcement points.

Primary sources:

- Smelting Foundry in Azuz: Festival Shares accumulate over hunts; claim them periodically.
- Smelting Foundry: Smelt Ore / smelt monster materials into reinforcement materials.
- Dismantle unwanted Artian weapons at Gemma via Reinforce/Dismantle Artian Weapons.
- Nata can provide/trade reinforcement materials.
- 9-star quests at HR100+ can reward Specklite, Argecite, and Oricalcite.
- Oricalcite can also appear from Weekly Bounties and smelting Gogmazios materials after Gogma progression.

Practical farming loop:

1. Claim Festival Shares at Azuz's Smelting Foundry.
2. Smelt excess monster/Gogma materials if you need points now.
3. Dismantle failed Artian rolls to recover reinforcement materials.
4. Run HR100+ 9-star quests while checking Nata/trader inventories between hunts.
