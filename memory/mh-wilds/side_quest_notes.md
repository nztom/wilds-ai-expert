# Monster Hunter Wilds Side Quest Notes

Last refreshed: 2026-05-08

Use `unlocks_and_special_items.md` for the broad side-mission unlock table. This file keeps walkthrough notes for side quests whose objectives are easy to misread or whose target is tucked away.

## Alma - The Apple of Her Eye

- Unlock context: HR24+ / Title Update 1 content.
- Goal: recover Alma's lost glasses in Scarlet Forest.
- Start by talking to Alma, then ask Wudwuds at Wudwud Hideout.
- First field clue: speak to Thunk near the Wudwud Hideout entrance; the quest marker points toward the river near Scarlet Forest Area 1 / base camp.
- Glasses-wearing Wudwud: in Scarlet Forest Area 2, in a small dark rock alcove along the narrow path east of Scampshroom / the first Wudwud clue. It may be near an amber deposit and is not clearly marked until approached.
- After the cutscene: return to Wudwud Hideout at night during Plenty and find Ayejack asleep in a cocoon bed on the lower level, in the side nook near Musharpeye's item-trader spot rather than the upper feast/platform area.
- Trade requirement: Eastern Honey, gathered from Giant Vigorwasps in Scarlet Forest.
