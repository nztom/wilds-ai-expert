# Monster Hunter Wilds Current Meta Notes

Last refreshed: 2026-05-06

These notes summarize the current High Rank / endgame build meta for the base-game window after Ver. 1.041.00.00, released February 18, 2026 UTC. Use this alongside the local CSV memory; for exact pieces, jewels, talismans, and slot pressure, query the normalized data files.

## Current Content Window

- Ver. 1.041 added Arch-Tempered Arkveld, 10-star Arch-Tempered versions of Rey Dau, Uth Duna, Nu Udra, and Jin Dahaad, permanent event quests, new equipment, and quality/performance fixes.
- Title Update 4 remains the main endgame build inflection point because it added Gogmazios, Gogma Artian weapons, Armor Transcendence, new talismans, Gogmapocalypse, and broad weapon buffs.
- Treat this as the final base-game meta snapshot until the announced large-scale expansion changes equipment, monsters, balance, or rank structure.

## Weapon Meta Snapshot

Game8's TU4-updated weapon tiering places the most broadly effective weapons as:

- SS: Light Bowgun, Dual Blades, Bow, Long Sword, Great Sword.
- S: Heavy Bowgun, Gunlance, Sword and Shield, Insect Glaive, Charge Blade.
- A: Hunting Horn, Hammer, Switch Axe.
- B: Lance.

Interpret this as a general endgame effectiveness and accessibility ranking, not a rule for individual hunters. Monster HunterMeta discussion and weapon-specialist builders still show strong results for lower-ranked weapons when the build is optimized and the player understands the matchup. For recommendations, prefer the user's chosen weapon first, then tune it toward the relevant damage model.

Important TU4 movement:

- Light Bowgun rose sharply after offensive buffs and remains a strong safe-DPS option.
- Dual Blades rose sharply because Demon Boost quality-of-life changes, higher elemental cap, and Gogma Artian weapons reward sustained elemental uptime.
- Hunting Horn improved after buffs but is still usually valued more for team utility than pure solo speed.
- Gunlance fell from the very top tier but remains strong and matchup-stable.
- Lance is ranked low in broad tier lists, but it still has a clear endgame niche for safe 10-star / Arch-Tempered play, Omega-style tanking, and Offensive Guard builds.

## Current Meta Armor Engines

- Gogmazios: Excellent mixed-set core. Chest, arms, waist, and greaves are especially relevant because Gogma pieces enable powerful group/set interactions such as Second Wind, Burst Boost, and Gogmapocalypse. Gogmapocalypse is especially valuable for elemental builds after TU4 elemental scaling buffs.
- Arkveld gamma: Strong Ver. 1.041 addition. Notable pieces include helm with Weakness Exploit 3, vambraces with Weakness Exploit 2 plus Flayer 2, mail with Convert Element 3, coil with Elemental Absorption 3, and greaves with Flayer 2 plus Blight Resistance 2. The set provides Arkveld's Hunger / Hasten Recovery and Lord's Soul / Guts (Tenacity). Its weakness is limited level-3 slot access.
- Gore Magala: Still a premier affinity engine because Black Eclipse plus Antivirus can provide large practical affinity. Builds using Gore pieces usually need Antivirus support to justify the set bonus.
- Rey Dau gamma / Uth Duna gamma / Nu Udra gamma / Dahaad gamma: Useful mixed pieces, often for Guts access, comfort, or high-value slots, but full sets are usually less efficient than mixed sets.
- Lagiacrus: Strong thunder and affinity package. Good source of Weakness Exploit and Maximum Might, but four-piece Azure Bolt can restrict build flexibility.
- Guardian Ebony / Guardian Fulgur: Useful transcended pieces for Burst Boost or Second Wind mixed sets.
- Zoh Shia: Comfort and elemental utility via Super Recovery, Coalescence, and Elemental Absorption. Strong when survivability matters more than absolute DPS.
- Omega / Bale / Seregios: More conditional engines. Omega is better in multiplayer and positional play; Bale is niche around True Shadowbringer / Dark Knight style builds; Seregios rewards evasion-heavy weapons and Bladescale play.

General rule: full sets are rarely ideal. Mixed sets usually win because they combine high-value individual skills, set/group breakpoints, talisman shortcuts, and Armor Transcendence slot upgrades.

## Current Meta Weapon Engines

- Gogma Artian weapons are the default endgame target. They upgrade Rarity 8 Artian weapons, keep reinforcement value, add an Attack / Affinity / Element focus, and roll one group skill plus one set bonus skill.
- Regular Artian weapons remain usable but should usually be upgraded into Gogma Artian versions once the hunter can farm Gogmazios.
- Zoh Shia weapons are good non-Artian alternatives, with high raw, white sharpness, acceptable affinity, and Whiteflame Torrent, especially into Gogmazios.
- Guardian Arkveld / Arkveld weapons are strong raw choices; Guardian variants can be excellent for non-crit or high-raw plans but require handling negative affinity.
- Lagiacrus weapons are strong Thunder options and useful into Thunder-weak matchups such as Omega Planetes and Uth Duna.
- Seregios weapons are good for evasion-heavy weapons/builds, but need consistent dodge-skill uptime.

## Gogma Artian Priorities

Focus choice is build-specific:

- Attack focus is usually the safe raw-DPS default if the build can absorb the affinity loss.
- Element focus is attractive for element-forward weapons and matched hunts because it adds element for only a small affinity penalty.
- Affinity focus is best when a build is not already near practical affinity cap; avoid overcapping after Weakness Exploit, Maximum Might, Antivirus, Azure Bolt, or weapon affinity.

Reroll targets:

- Group skill: Lord's Soul is broadly prized because Guts (Tenacity) is strong in 9-star, 10-star, and Arch-Tempered content. Lord's Fury and other weapon-specific group options can be good when they complete an armor plan.
- Set bonus: choose the bonus that unlocks the mixed set. Common high-value targets include Gore Magala's Tyranny, Leviathan's Fury, Seregios's Tenacity, Jin Dahaad's Revolt, Fulgur Anjanath's Will, Ebony Odogaron's Power, Omega Resonance, Soul of the Dark Knight, and Gogmapocalypse.
- Reinforcement priority is normally Attack / Element / Sharpness first, then Affinity only if the build is affinity-starved.

## Skill Heuristics

Build from mandatory weapon-side skills first, then armor-side offense and comfort:

- Raw melee: Attack Boost, Agitator, Weakness Exploit, Critical Boost, Critical Eye or Maximum Might, Burst, Counterstrike, Peak Performance/Resentment if uptime is realistic, sharpness support as needed.
- Element melee: weapon element, Critical Element, Burst, Weakness Exploit, affinity engine, sharpness support, Convert Element / Elemental Absorption when the armor and matchup support it.
- Bow / Dual Blades: prioritize element, affinity, Critical Element, stamina economy, then raw/comfort. TU4 and Gogma Artian favor element-forward play.
- Bowguns: start with ammo-type damage, recoil/reload/ammo economy, then attack/affinity. LBG is especially strong after TU4 buffs; HBG remains high damage but demands stronger positioning.
- LBG Pierce note, refreshed 2026-05-07: current guides treat Pierce as the best general/base LBG ammo because it keeps Normal-like handling while gaining multi-hit damage and Rapid Fire gauge recovery. Elemental LBG remains the higher-ceiling prepared-matchup option, while Pierce is the flexible raw/generalist choice. Pierce builds commonly prioritize Pierce Jewel, Ballistics/Precise, Opening Shot, Rapid Fire support when the gun rapid-fires Pierce, and Pierce Ammo Magazine where available. In Wilds, "Rapid Fire" is not the old always-on World-style passive burst; compatible ammo can rapid-fire only in Rapid Fire Mode, which uses the Rapid Fire gauge and has its own mode actions such as Rapid Chaser Shot and Burst Step. Sources: Game8 LBG weapon guide / HR LBG builds, Mobalytics TU4 LBG meta, Wiggler.pet LBG guide.
- Bethorned Agony / Gogma Artian LBG capacity note, refreshed 2026-05-07: capacity increases are incremental. Bowgun mods can add +1 clip size for a chosen ammo magazine, Rapid Fire Magazine adds +1 Rapid Fire Mode capacity, and Gogma Artian Ammo Boost reinforcement is listed as +1 / +2. Gogma LBG Element Focus adds capacity to compatible elemental/status ammo, but Affinity Focus mainly gives Rapid Fire compatibility for Normal/Pierce. Do not expect a large universal magazine increase from decorations; Load Shells/Magazine jewels are for Gunlance/Charge Blade, not bowgun ammo.
- Guard weapons: Guard, Guard Up when matchup requires it, Offensive Guard, then raw/crit/sharpness. Do not blindly over-invest in guard if perfect guard and matchup knowledge cover the need.
- Hard quests: Guts (Tenacity), Divine Blessing, relevant resistances, Stun Resistance, Blight Resistance, and Super Recovery can beat small sheet-DPS gains if they prevent carts or lost uptime.

## Matchup Notes

- 10-star / Arch-Tempered content rewards defensive breakpoints and uptime more than fragile max-DPS sets. Guts (Tenacity) is a major meta comfort skill here.
- Omega Planetes is role-driven: Lance/Gunlance tank packages, SnS/Hunting Horn support, and Thunder DPS from Lagiacrus-style weapons are common recommendations.
- Gogmazios is Dragon-weak and has a Fire-softening interaction. Consider Zoh Shia or Dragon-focused Gogma Artian builds, but do not ignore raw uptime and survivability.
- For Arch-Tempered Arkveld, Arkveld gamma armor is both the reward and a strong post-clear mixed-set option; before farming it, lean on Gogma, Gore, gamma apex pieces, and defensive talisman shortcuts.
